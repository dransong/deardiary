MEETINGS

The following meetings are considered compulsory for all members;
  1. Board Meeting
  2. Annual General Meeting
  3. Meetings of committees and Units
  4. Meetings of Branches
If for any reason you have to absent yourself, the following applies;
  1. If the reason for which one must absent himself or herself is known prior to the meeting, the individual must seek permission at least a day before the meeting from the organizer of the meeting.

  2. If the reason wasn't known prior to the date of the meeting or an emergency situation prevents you from joining the meeting you will explain yourself on or a day after the meeting.
You shall not absent yourself from two(2) consecutive meetings and/or 5 meetings within the year.

BENEFITS AND RIGHTS

  • You shall benefit from various training sessions organised by the Organization on life skills and self-improvement.

  • You shall benefit from networking with others in the Organization.
